================================================================
  COLOR OVERHAUL: 7 THEMES + NEW MATCHUP CSV
================================================================

A full color-system overhaul. The renderer now resolves colors
through a theme module, with 7 presets to pick from in the UI.

================================================================
  THEMES
================================================================

CSV Default (recommended):
  Uses the curated matchup CSV (992 rows, 4 colors per matchup).
  Each matchup gets a hand-tuned palette - away accent, home
  accent, banner color, and background.

Classic Bordered:
  The original look. Bright team primaries with auto-swap when
  both teams have similar primaries (e.g. NYR + BUF both blue ->
  BUF gets gold).

Midnight:
  Deep navy bg + gold accents everywhere. Award-show premium.

Ice Rink:
  Dark blue tinted bg, light blue chrome, team colors on stats.
  Cold arctic feel.

Heritage:
  Sepia/warm vintage. Aged-paper bg, brown chrome, muted team
  accents, cream text. Classic broadcast vibe.

Neon:
  Pure black bg with electric brights. Cyan chrome, team colors
  at full saturation. Arcade aesthetic.

Stealth:
  Graphite gray bg, monochrome chrome, team colors only on
  scores and stats values. Tactical/minimal.

================================================================
  HOW TO PICK A THEME
================================================================

In the app's Theme panel (left sidebar), click any of the 7
preset cards at the top. The selection is saved to the project
file as `color_theme`.

Detail color overrides at the bottom of the panel still work
for fine-tuning a specific element on top of any theme.

================================================================
  HOW TO RE-UPLOAD
================================================================

1. Unzip somewhere temporary.
2. View -> Show -> Hidden items so .github folder is visible.
3. Drag everything inside the unzipped folder onto your local
   nhl-scoreboard folder. Replace all when asked.
4. GitHub Desktop:
     Summary: "color overhaul: 7 themes + new matchup CSV"
     Commit + Push
5. Wait ~10 min for the new .msi.
